At HBS

In 2012 I was hired by HBS Professors Josh Lerner and Matthew Rhodes-Kropf to develop course materials for a private equity and venture capital course for second year MBA students. In this position, I developed algorithms and financial models for a venture capital and private equity market simulation, the first of its kind, that enables MBA students to play the role of a virtual private equity firm. Within the simulation, students raise funds, search for companies to invest in, complete deals, and manage a portfolio of companies across a number of simulated years.
